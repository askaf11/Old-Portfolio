A simple way to start

The website is one of the most important things. You can start a business, organization, restaurant, or personal brand without a website, but it is not fully complete. Because you can showcase your products, services, workflow, ideology, and more on your website. As a result, the website is essential for anything you can do.

Website requirements

  • Domain
  • Hosting
  • SSL certificate (Based on Hosting)
  • Professional Email (Based on your preference)

Efficient way to start

The first thing you should do is invest your money in the right place. Because websites have different categories like personal, business, and e-commerce with various plans and pricing, you can find one that is ideal for you and then invest your money in it. And next, your website cannot initially get a high volume of traffic. So you don't spend excessive money in the initial period of the website. Keep all this in mind and invest a reasonable amount of money in the website.

Initially, we can use a free resource to avoid a lot of expenses. After that, if your website experiences a high volume of traffic or your business growth increases, you can upgrade your website plans.

Domain registration is compulsory for websites because it’s the identity of your website, and it’s not free; you always have to pay money on a subscription basis. The domain expenses are based on your domain extension and domain providers. The domain extension means ".com," ".in," or something similar.

Hosting and email are free forever with some restrictions. You can use the initial stage because that provider is giving low-capacity storage and specifications, and if your website traffic exceeds the free resource capacity, you can upgrade everything based on your needs.

Summary

If you want to have your own website, Only spend money on a domain name. then hosting and email can be used as free resources. You can use those free resources for a period of time.
